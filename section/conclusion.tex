\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}
    In this paper, we presented a foundational design and implementation of a remote control system for train operations, leveraging the capabilities of WebTransport and the deterministic execution model of Lingua Franca. Our architecture integrates real-time video streaming and low-latency control command transmission, demonstrating the feasibility of using modern web protocols to meet the latency requirements of remote control system. The modular design of our system allows for scalability and adaptability to various operational scenarios, while the use of Lingua Franca ensures deterministic behavior under real world environments. Our evaluation results from real world tests indicate the feasibility of using WebTransport. Future work will focus on further optimizing the communication protocols, enhancing the user interface for remote operators, and conducting more extensive field tests in real-world railway environments and measure the timing requirements for different reactions and overall performance of the LF based train client.
